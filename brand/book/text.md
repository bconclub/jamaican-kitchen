===== PAGE 7 =====
B R A N D  G U I D E L I N E S
Do's & don'ts
  
✓DO
• Use the phrase “Bold Jamaican Flavor”
• Keep it clean, modern and food-focused
• Show real food — real portions, texture & color
• Make online ordering easy and fast
• Make catering feel professional and reliable
• Use warm, confident, welcoming language
• Honor Jamaican roots without feeling limiting
• Stay consistent across every touchpoint
  
✕DON'T
• No tourist-style Caribbean theme
• Don't overuse palm trees, beaches or stereotypes
• Don't clutter the site or complicate ordering
• No weak photography or stock imagery
• Tone never too formal or too casual
• Never make the food feel intimidating
• Don't box in menu innovation or growth
• Don't lose the Jamaican cultural influence
