# Jamaican Kitchen — Information We Need From You

To set up your backend and website correctly, we need the details below. The brand look & feel is
done — this is the **operational content** that powers your menu, ordering, and dashboard.

You can send it any way that's easy (spreadsheet, doc, photos in a folder). Templates are below.

---

## 1. Your menu (the main thing)

For **every item** you sell, we need:

| Field | Example |
|---|---|
| Item name | Oxtail |
| Short description | Tender, slow-cooked oxtail in rich brown gravy with butter beans |
| Category | Mains / Patties / Seafood / Sides / Drinks… |
| Price | $18.99 |
| Photo | A clear, good-quality photo of the dish (one per item) |
| Spice level | Mild / Medium / Hot |
| Starting stock | How many you typically have ready (e.g. 40) |
| Best-seller? | Yes / No (these get featured on the homepage) |

> Easiest format: one row per item in a spreadsheet, plus a folder of photos named after each item.

---

## 2. Add-ons & extras

For items that have options, tell us the choices and their prices:
- **Required choices** (e.g. "Pick a side: rice & peas / plain rice / festival")
- **Optional extras** (e.g. "Add extra plantain +$2", "Extra gravy +$1")
- How many can be picked (pick 1, pick up to 2, etc.)

> List these next to the item they belong to.

---

## 3. Stock & "sold out" rules

- The **starting stock** per item (from section 1).
- The **low-stock alert level** — when should the dashboard warn you it's running low? (e.g. alert at 10 left)
- Confirm the rule: when an item hits **0**, it shows as **Sold out** on the website automatically.
  Staff can also manually switch any item **on/off** anytime.

---

## 4. Your 6 locations

For each location, confirm:
- Address, phone, opening hours (we have a draft — just confirm/correct)
- **Does the menu differ by location?** i.e. does every location serve the same items at the same
  prices, or do some items / prices change per location?

---

## 5. Catering menu

Same idea as the main menu, but for catering trays:
- Catering item name + description + photo
- Tray sizes and prices (e.g. Small tray feeds 8–12 = $X, Large feeds 18–22 = $Y)
- Any rules (minimum guests, how much advance notice you need)

---

## 6. Coupons / promo codes *(optional — only if you want them)*

If you'd like discount codes, tell us for each one:
- The code (e.g. JERK10)
- The discount (10% off, or $5 off)
- Minimum order to qualify (if any)
- Start & end dates
- Any limits (one use per customer, total uses)
- What it applies to (whole order / specific items / specific location)

## 7. Reviews *(optional)*

How would you like customer reviews handled?
- Collect new reviews on the website (you approve them before they show), **and/or**
- Show your existing Google reviews, **and/or**
- We add a curated set you provide.

---

## 8. Team access (dashboard logins)

List the people who need access to the dashboard (up to 10):

| Name | Email | Access level | Location(s) |
|---|---|---|---|
| e.g. Milton Jackson | owner@… | Owner (everything) | All |
| e.g. Shop manager | manager@… | Shop Manager (one store) | Vernon |

- **Owner** — full access, all locations, including financials
- **Admin** — day-to-day operations
- **Shop Manager** — limited to their assigned location(s)

---

## 9. Brand assets *(mostly done — just confirming)*

- Logo files ✅ (have them)
- Final brand colors ✅
- Licensed fonts — please send when ready (we're using close stand-ins until then)
- Food photography — the per-item photos from section 1 are the main thing still needed

---

### Quickest path
1. Fill the **menu spreadsheet** (sections 1–3) + drop **photos** in a folder.
2. Confirm **locations** (section 4) and send the **catering** list (section 5).
3. Tell us about **coupons / reviews** (6–7) only if you want them.
4. Send the **team list** (section 8) and any **remaining brand files** (section 9).

Once we have these, we wire it all into Dine Central and it syncs straight to the website.
